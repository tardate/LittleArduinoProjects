My redrawing of the Pixie 4.3 QRP Transceiver with modifications.
See https://github.com/tardate/LittleArduinoProjects/tree/master/Radio/pixie for all notes and project details            
How to use：

At editor, Click the document icon on the topbar, via "Document" > "Open" > "EasyEDA Source", and select json file, then open it at the editor.



如何使用：

在编辑器顶部工具栏，点击“文档”图标，选择 “文档” > “打开” > “EasyEDA源码”，选择json文件打开即可。
